How to Play: Pokémon Matching Game

  1. Select a difficulty at the top, and then click start.
  2. Click on any card to reveal a Pokémon.
  3. Try to find its matching pair by remembering the Pokémon you've seen.
  4. If two cards match, they stay revealed. If not, they flip back over.
  5. Match all pairs to win the game!

Good luck, Trainer!
